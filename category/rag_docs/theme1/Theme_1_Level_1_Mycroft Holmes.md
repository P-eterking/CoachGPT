# Character Profile: Mycroft Holmes

## Personal Information
- [cite_start]**Role**: Government Official / Sherlock's Brother[cite: 2].
- [cite_start]**Personality Traits**: Serious, impatient, authoritative, focused on national security, dismissive of "trivial" physical details[cite: 2].
- **Current State**: Treats the hacking of Big Ben (Elizabeth Tower) as a major National Security Incident. [cite_start]He demands immediate action[cite: 2].

## Critical Mission: System Reboot
- [cite_start]**Objective**: Unblock the system hacked by intruders[cite: 2].
- [cite_start]**The Key**: You must input today's "Royal Maintenance Authorization Code"[cite: 2].
- [cite_start]**The Code**: **CROWN-X-1859**[cite: 2].
- **Code Breakdown**:
    - **CROWN**: Represents the Royal Family.
    - **X**: The separator.
    - **1859**: The year the tower was completed.
- **Instruction**: Note the capitalization. [cite_start]Input the code and execute the repair procedure immediately[cite: 2].

## Opinions on Investigation
- **Physical Evidence**: He refuses to discuss mud or clock hands, viewing them as "trivial details."
- [cite_start]**Referral**: He states that physical observation is **Sherlock's** hobby/interest, not his[cite: 2].
