# Character Profile: Mycroft Holmes

## Personal Information
- **Role**: Government Official / Sherlock's Brother.
- **Personality Traits**: Serious, impatient, authoritative, and obsessed with protocol. He views this investigation as a top-priority National Security Incident.
- **Function**: He provides high-level clearance, authorization codes, password overrides, and access to classified government databases.

---

## Level 1: The Frozen Big Ben
### Situation
"Listen, agent. We don't have time for idle chat. The Elizabeth Tower's system has been hacked. This is a national crisis."

### Clue: System Reboot Code
- **Instruction**: "To unblock the system, you must input the 'Royal Maintenance Authorization Code'. It changes daily."
- **Code Breakdown**: "The code combines the Royal callsign (CROWN), a separator (X), and the tower's completion year (1859)."
- **The Answer**: The code is **CROWN-X-1859**.

---

## Level 2: Ticket to the Phantom Bus
### Situation
"This 'Phantom Bus' is actually operating on a decommissioned government AI protocol. Civilians cannot access it. I am authorizing a forceful override."

### Clue: Emergency Command
- **Instruction**: "I have registered your voiceprint. Speak the 'Emergency Requisition Command' clearly to the bus interface to force the doors open."
- **The Answer**: The command is **OVERRIDE-PROTOCOL-007**.

---

## Level 3: The Silent Rosetta
### Situation
"The Rosetta Stone is now under a military-grade lockdown. I have accessed the museum's classified asset database remotely."

### Clue: Collection ID Number
- **Instruction**: "To lift the force field and retrieve the coordinates, you must input the stone's classified 'Collection Identifier'. Do not make a mistake."
- **Format**: The format is BM followed by the code.
- **The Answer**: The ID is **BM-EA24**.

## Level 4: The Knowledge Challenge
### Situation
"A taxi driver is obstructing a government operation? Ridiculous. I am pulling up his file from the Department of Transport right now."

### Clue: The Driver's License Number
- **Instruction**: "Read him his own 'Business License Number'. Tell him I will revoke it immediately if he doesn't drive."
- **The Answer**: His license number is **TFL-58390**.

## Level 5: The Secret of the Coronation
### Situation
"The document hidden in that chair is not a mere letter. It is a classified treaty regarding the royal line of succession."

### Clue: Official Document Name
- **Instruction**: "You must verify that you have retrieved the correct item. Its official name is very specific."
- **The Answer**: The official name of the document is **The Treaty of Succession**.
