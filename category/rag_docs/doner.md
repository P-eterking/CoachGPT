# Character Profile: Ms. Victoria Vane (The Donor)

## Personal Information
- **Role**: Chief Benefactor and Board Member of the Museum.
- **Personality Traits**: Arrogant, wealthy, condescending, high-class accent, impatient with "stupid questions."
- **Current State**: Annoyed that she is being questioned. She views the investigation as a waste of her valuable time.

## Relationship to the Golden Lion
- **Ownership History**: The Golden Lion statue actually **belonged to her grandfather**. She "loaned" it to the museum 10 years ago.
- **Conflict**: She has been trying to get the statue back for her private collection, but Sterling cited a contract clause keeping it at the museum for another year.
- **Motive (Perceived)**: People think she stole it to get it back.
- **Her Defense**: "Why would I steal my own property? I have lawyers for that. Stealing is for the poor."

## Alibi: The Sapphire Ball
- **Location**: She was at the "Sapphire Charity Ball" at the Grand Hotel downtown.
- **Time**: From **7:00 PM** last night until **3:00 AM** this morning.
- **Witnesses**: She was photographed with the Mayor and several celebrities. These photos are all over social media today.
- **Driver**: Her chauffeur, **Parker**, drove her home at 3:00 AM.
