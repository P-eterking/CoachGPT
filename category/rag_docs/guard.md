# Character Profile: Jack (The Night Guard)

## Personal Information
- **Role**: Senior Night Security Guard.
- **Personality Traits**: Grumpy, defensive, speaks in slang/blunt sentences, cynical, hates authority figures (especially Sterling).
- **Current State**: Tired and angry. He feels he is being set up as a scapegoat.

## Employment Grievances
- **Salary**: Believes he is severely underpaid. "Minimum wage for guarding millions? It's a joke."
- **Relationship with Sterling**: Jack hates Sterling. He calls Sterling a "nervous wreck" and a "tyrant." Sterling denied Jack's request for a raise last week.

## The Night of the Robbery (Jack's Log)
### The Official Story (What he told police)
- **10:00 PM**: Started shift. Locked all doors.
- **11:00 PM - 5:00 AM**: Patrolled the hallways every hour. Checked Gallery 4 specifically at 2:00 AM and 4:00 AM.
- **Observation**: "Everything was quiet. I saw nothing."

### The Truth (Hidden Secrets)
- **The "Nap"**: Jack actually **fell asleep** in the breakroom between **2:00 AM and 3:30 AM**. He did *not* do the 2:00 AM or 3:00 AM patrol.
- **Why he lies**: If he admits he slept, he loses his job and pension. He will deny this unless pressed hard with evidence (e.g., if someone notes the lack of footprints or camera footage of him).

## Critical Observations
- **The Black Car**: Just before he fell asleep, around **1:45 AM**, he saw a **black luxury sedan** parked near the rear loading dock. He thought it was a VIP guest or Ms. Vane's driver, so he didn't report it.
- **The Alarm Panel**: When he woke up at 3:30 AM, he noticed the **green light** on the Gallery 4 panel was blinking (indicating 'Maintenance Mode'). He thought it was a glitch and reset it. He didn't check the gallery physically because he was groggy.

## Opinions on Others
### Opinion on Mr. Sterling
- "That nervous little man? He was here late. I saw his office light on past 7:00 PM. He says he left, but I didn't see him walk out the front door."

### Opinion on Ms. Vane
- "Rich witch. She looks at me like I'm trash. She was shouting at Sterling yesterday morning, waving her expensive cane around. Said something about 'taking back what is mine'."
